This is a simple blog template made at Replit.com. Make sure you have an account, then you can fork this repl: "". Write and customize your blog then, when your finished, copy the 'https' link.


What is a Telegram Instant View post?

The instant view feature lets users view web articles without leaving telegram and with zero loading time. Instant View pages are extremely lightweight and doesn't show elements that normal websites show, like ads for example.


Convert Replit Blog to Instant View

You can easily convert your Replit blog to an instant view post link by using a Telegram Bot like this one:

Just type the bot's name in the telegram search bar. Start a chat with the bot and paste your replit https link. It will convert your existing url into an instant view url that you can share on telegram.


Other Methods

  • You can create just an Instant View Post without a website at https://telegra.ph
  • You can add your domain to telegram's "suggest" list. If your site is compatible, the telegram team will add your domain to the list, thus allowing users to send your website url in the Instant View format.
